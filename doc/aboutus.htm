About Us

The Zrato team is a small group of equity analysts and java programmers. We have been in the business of designing automated trading systems for 15 years. Our successful track record in trading the financial markets has allowed us the freedom to design a system based on over 33 years of investment experience. We placed our first trade in 1971, and are still as passionate now as we were then about the complexity and fascination of the market.

Our goal is to help you better understand what is going on in the equity markets by coming up with algorithms that sorts through data on thousands of companies and reduces it down to investment opportunities that are tailored to your way of thinking about the markets.
